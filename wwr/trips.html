Our trips here in Mexico Rafting Veracruz:

1. Adventure Enthusiasts' Expedition

"Ride the Rapids: Unleash Your Inner Explorer!"

Are you an adrenaline junkie looking for the ultimate rush? Join us on the Adventure Enthusiasts' Expedition! Navigate the wild waters of Veracruz and conquer challenging rapids. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers who want an unforgettable rafting experience.

Price: $99 per person

Connection to Train Maya: Convenient Train Maya access ensures you reach our base camp swiftly from major cities in Mexico.

Family Fun Float

"Wholesome Family Adventures Await!"

Embark on a journey of a lifetime with your loved ones! Our Family Fun Float is designed for families, friends, and nature lovers. Enjoy a leisurely rafting experience through scenic routes, suitable for all ages. Create lasting memories with your loved ones amidst Veracruz's stunning landscapes.

$79 per person, $69 for children under 12

Train Maya offers family-friendly travel options, bringing you directly to our family rafting adventure.

Romantic Riverside Rendezvous

"Love & Adventure: A Match Made in Veracruz"

For couples seeking a romantic escape, our Romantic Riverside Rendezvous is the perfect choice. Drift along the serene river, surrounded by lush greenery and the sounds of nature. This intimate rafting experience sets the stage for an unforgettable romantic outing.

$149 per couple

Train Maya's idyllic journey leads to the perfect beginning of your romantic adventure.

Eco-Explorer's Discovery

"Eco-Friendly Adventure for Conservationists"

Calling all eco-conscious travelers! Join our Eco-Explorer's Discovery and delve into Veracruz's rich biodiversity. Our guides will provide insight into the local ecosystems and conservation efforts as you raft through protected areas. This trip is an eco-friendly way to connect with nature and promote sustainability.

$119 per person

Train Maya promotes eco-friendly travel, ensuring you reach our ecotourism location with minimal environmental impact.
